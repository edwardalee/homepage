Plato and the Nerd:
The Creative Partnership of
Humans and Technology
MIT Press, 2017

In this book, Lee argues that technology has advanced to the point where its progress is limited not by physical constraints but by the human imagination, and that our aim for artificial intelligence to reproduce human cognitive function vastly underestimates the potential of computers. He goes on to argue that technology is co-evolving with humans - that while it augments our cognitive and physical capabilities, we nurture, develop, and propagate technology itself.
