Philosophy of Technology Papers

Although this is neither up-to-date nor comprehensive, below is a list of selected papers about philosophy of technology, including digital humanism, AI and sentience, and the relationship between humans and machines.

Books

  2. Edward Ashford Lee, Plato and the Nerd: The Creative Partnership of Humans and Technology, MIT Press, 2017. (Open access)
